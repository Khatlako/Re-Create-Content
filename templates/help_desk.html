Help Desk

Welcome to the Econet Business Help Desk. Here you can find answers to common questions and get in touch with our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I create an account? – Click on the Signup button in the top navigation and fill in your details.
  • How can I upload content? – Navigate to your dashboard and select "Upload" to submit files or posts.
  • How do I check pending or approved content? – Use the "Pending" or "Approved" sections in your dashboard sidebar.
  • Can I change my subscription? – Visit the "Settings" page to manage your subscription plans.
